Yuyang WANG

yuyangwang (at) hkust-gz.edu.cn



I am an Assistant Professor in the CMA Thrust, Information Hub at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou) Information Hub.

From 2022 to 2024, I worked as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in CMA. I received a PhD in computer science from the Arts et Métiers Institute of Technology, ParisTech in France. Under the framework of the French‐German Institute for Industry of the Future, I was a visiting researcher at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology in Germany. Additionally, I hold a master's degree in numerical methods in engineering from the International Center for Numerical Methods (CIMNE) at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia in Spain.

Recruitment

The group offers sufficient funding, abundant computing resources, and close collaborations with domain researchers. The research group is actively seeking highly motivated PhD, Red Bird MPhil and RA with strong backgrounds in computer science, data science, electronic engineering, or related fields.

  • RedBird MPhil students have the opportunity to develop their research skills with hands-on mentoring, and successful candidates are well-positioned for our PhD program, with an advantage over external applicants.
  • PhD should have solid research experience aligning with our research interests.
  • Applicants with limited research experience, joining our group as RA can be a valuable option.

Solid programming and/or mathematical skills are required.

To apply, please send an email to me, attaching your CV along with highlighted strengths and qualifications.
