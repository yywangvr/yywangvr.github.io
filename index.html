Yuyang WANG

yuyangwang (at) hkust-gz.edu.cn



I am an Assistant Professor in the Computational Media and Arts (CMA) Thrust at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou) Information Hub.

I was with the CMA thrust from 2022 to 2024, where he held the position of Postdoctoral Research Fellow. I received a PhD in computer science from the Arts et Métiers Institute of Technology, ParisTech in France. Under the framework of the French‐German Institute for Industry of the Future, I was a visiting researcher at the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology in Germany. Additionally, I hold a master's degree in numerical methods in engineering from the International Center for Numerical Methods (CIMNE) at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia in Spain.

Recruitment

  • PhD & Mphil students
  • Research assistants & Interns (those during gap year or seeking research training)

RedBird MPhil students in my group have the opportunity to develop their research skills with hands-on mentoring, and successful candidates are well-positioned for admission to our PhD program, with an advantage over direct applicants. Interested students are encouraged to reach out.
PhD applicants should have solid research experience aligning with our research interests.
Applicants with limited research experience, joining our group as an intern or research assistant can be a valuable option.

Research Interests

  • Explainable AI in Neuroscience
  • Computer Vision/Reinforcement Learning
  • AI for Good

Computer skills
